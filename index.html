Saturday, the 27th June, was the Global Pride Day and, if it weren't for the pandemic, I would have joined the Milan pride parade. hen the parade was cancelled I felt a little bad, but what could they do? I've never been to a pride on purpose (last year I was on a studying holiday in Dublin and when we went in Belfast for a trip we saw the pride - even if nobody knew there would have been it). By the way, I didn't really join the parade, I just saw it, but there I decided I would have joined the Milan pride in 2020. I was excited to see the Virtual Global Pride, but I didn't like it: I saw just about an hour of the pride, but it seemed to be badly organized to me, too divided. Every person who made a speech spoke for about a minute and then appeared another person who told other different things. And the songs: they were put almost casually in the video... I don't know, in the official website it was described and i expected something way better. Now I hope I'll be at the Milan pride next year :) .
